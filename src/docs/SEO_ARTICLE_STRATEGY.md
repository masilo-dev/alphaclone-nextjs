## SEO Strategy

### For Each Article:
- **Title**: Keyword-rich, 50-60 characters
- **Meta Description**: 150-160 characters, includes CTA
- **Keywords**: 5-10 relevant keywords
- **Content**: 1500-2500 words minimum
- **Internal Links**: Link to your services
- **External Links**: 2-3 authoritative sources
- **Images**: 2-3 optimized images with alt text
- **Schema Markup**: Article schema included
- **CTA**: "Contact AlphaClone Systems" at the end

### URL Structure:
```

/blog/react-vs-vue-vs-angular-comparison
```
